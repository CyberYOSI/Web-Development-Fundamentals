Design

The aesthetic for Cineverse is designed to reflect the scholarly, archival nature of the content. The layout is clean, high-contrast, and focused on readability and historical context.

Category Description
Design The site uses a Dark Theme to copy a cinematic viewing environment and to make the rich, colourful classic film posters pop. The primary typeface for body text will be a Georgia to enhance readability
Usability Navigation and Filtering are paramount. A fixed navigation bar ensures easy movement between sections. allowing users to the relevant to film history. Line height and paragraph spacing will be added to reduce reading fatigue.
Colour Palette The palette is limited to ensure sophistication and focus:
  • Primary Background: Deep Charcoal Grey (#121212)
  • Primary Text: White/Light Silver (#E0E0E0)
  • Accent Colour: Muted Gold (#B8860B) used for titles, links, and borders
